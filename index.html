HackStorm: The Power BI Hackathon

HackStorm is an intense, real-time Power BI competition where participants race against the clock to clean, analyze, and visualize data. Designed to mimic real-world business challenges, this event tests participants' ability to transform raw datasets into actionable insights through efficient data cleaning, interactive dashboards, and compelling storytelling.

The Data Storyteller: Turning Numbers into Narratives

The Data Storyteller challenges participants to go beyond dashboards and craft compelling, insight-driven narratives. By blending data analysis with storytelling, this competition pushes participants to transform raw numbers into meaningful stories that drive action and decision-making.

Power BI Bingo: Gamifying Data Analytics

Power BI Bingo transforms data analysis into a fast-paced, strategic game where creativity and technical skills collide. Participants tackle unconventional Power BI challenges in a Bingo-style format, pushing them to think quickly, experiment boldly, and turn data into a fun, competitive experience.
